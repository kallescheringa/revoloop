Our Services

At Revoloop, we make it easy to return your used PLA 3D printing material. We collect from makers, schools, businesses, and hobbyists.

For every kilogram of PLA you return, you receive instant store credit. Earn while contributing to a circular economy!

Calculate Your Credit

How It Works

1. Collect your used PLA filament or leftover material.

2. Schedule a pickup through our website or drop it off at a collection point.

3. We weigh your material and calculate your store credit.

4. You receive your credit immediately and help close the loop sustainably.
